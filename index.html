About Us

The RadioTech project was created in 2022 by 3 young people.

The main problem solved by the project is that the persons conducting radiation monitoring are affected by radiation. To eliminate this problem, we have developed an operational measurement method. Thus, the project eliminates human labor and its resulting errors in parallel with the elimination of damage to human health. It has several unique differences from other projects:

1.All components in the device are controlled by an automatic system.
2.It is more profitable.
3.Even in a high-radiation area, the device is able to carry out monitoring.
